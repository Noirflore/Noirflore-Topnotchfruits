Why NoirFlorè exists

Built in Perth by a founder who was tired of “standard” hampers. NoirFlorè is what happens when you mix dried fruit obsession with luxury packaging.

Most fruit gifts look like an afterthought. We wanted something that felt like a luxury candle or a small bottle of niche perfume – but with fruit.

  • Rigid magnetic-lid boxes instead of flimsy cardboard.
  • Gold foil branding and matte black, not supermarket colours.
  • Small-batch drying so fruit tastes clean, not burnt or sugary.
  • Freeze n’ Pop cans that feel fun and modern, not old-school tubs.
