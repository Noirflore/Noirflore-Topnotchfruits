Reality checks (the good kind)

A few honest notes so you know exactly what to expect when you order from NoirFlorè.

We are small-batch, not a factory

Every box is sliced, dried and packed in short runs. Some fruits sell out and occasionally a flavour will be unavailable. If we can’t hit the standard we want, we’d rather refund than send an average box.

Real fruit means real variation

Colour, shape and sweetness change with the season. Your box will never look copy-paste identical to a photo – that’s the whole point. You’re getting real fruit, not candy.

Orders are checked by a human

When you place an order we review it manually, confirm stock and delivery timing, and only then send a secure Stripe payment link from our business email.

Clear communication

You’ll always know where your order is up to. If anything is delayed or not available, we’ll tell you directly and offer options – swap, delay, or refund.

Why NoirFlorè exists

Built by a founder who was tired of “standard” hampers. NoirFlorè is what happens when you mix dried fruit obsession with luxury packaging.

Most fruit gifts look like an afterthought. We wanted something that felt like a luxury candle or a small bottle of niche perfume – but with fruit.

  • Rigid magnetic-lid boxes instead of flimsy cardboard.
  • Gold foil branding and matte black, not supermarket colours.
  • Small-batch drying so fruit tastes clean, not burnt or sugary.
  • Freeze n’ Pop cans that feel fun and modern, not old-school tubs.
